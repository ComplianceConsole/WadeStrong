WHAT IS RPE?

RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) is a 1–10 scale of how hard a set actually was — specifically how many reps you had left when you stopped. Wade programs everything by RPE because it accounts for how you're actually feeling on the day, not just what a spreadsheet says.

6

Easy

4+ reps left. Warm-up weight. Could keep going all day.

7

Moderate

3 reps left. Working but comfortable. Good for volume days.

8

Hard

2 reps left. Where most main work lives. Challenging but sustainable.

9

Very hard

1 rep left, maybe. Near your limit. Peak week territory.

10

Absolute max

Zero reps left. Nothing more. Your true 1RM.
