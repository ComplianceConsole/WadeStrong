Common questions

FAQ

Do I need a Hyrox-specific gym?
+
No. The program is designed for commercial gyms. Where Hyrox equipment isn't available, Wade programs practical substitutions. You still do the simulation session every week.
I've never done a Hyrox before. Is this suitable?
+
Yes. The program is built for first-timers through to experienced competitors chasing a time improvement. Your questionnaire captures your current fitness level and Wade programs accordingly.
How many days per week do I need to train?
+
Typically 4 days per week. Wade can scale to 3 days if that's what your schedule allows, and up to 5–6 for more advanced athletes.
Open or Pro — which division should I enter?
+
Open is the standard entry point. Pro is significantly heavier. Wade will advise based on your questionnaire if you're unsure. Most first-timers should race Open.
I already have a race booked. Will 6 weeks be enough?
+
6 weeks is enough to make a meaningful improvement to your race performance. If your race is sooner, note that in the questionnaire and Wade will adjust the program to peak you at the right time.
What happens after I pay?
+
After payment you'll be sent a link to the Hyrox questionnaire. Wade reviews your answers and builds your program within 24 hours. You'll receive a link to your client portal where the full 6-week program lives.
